Konza ABC Secondary School Eco Club

Claudia Mbinda Mwende

Weekly ongoing programs at Konza ABC Secondary School Eco Club include: 

  • Tree and flower nurseries
  • Compost production
  • Reuse of waste plastic bags for potted plants
  • Ecological education
  • Talent and Art:-comedy,music,poetry,Acrobatics
  • Tree planting along the school fence 

 

Goals

 Education that addresses unemployment, food security,conservation ,best management of natural resources;A next generetion that understands its role in environmental stewardship in an era of climate change.

Using talent to address idleness and rampant unemployment that has been rated a national crisis.
